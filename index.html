Crystal Collectors

Instructions

At the start of the game, you will be given a random number. Located below the number, there are four crystals. Clicking on a crystal will add a specified amount of points to your total score.You will be able to win the game by matching your score, with the number found in the beginning of the game. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the value of the crystal will change.
